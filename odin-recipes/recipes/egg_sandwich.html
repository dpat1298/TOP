Egg Sandwich

Description

Here is a simple but delicious breakfast meal that can be eaten on the go. Perfect for breakfast on the way to work.

Ingredients

  • 2 slices of bread of your choice or an English Muffin
  • 1 egg
  • Your choice of sausage patty or bacon strips
  • Mayonaise
  • Your choice of spicy sause
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Steps

  1. Toast bread or english muffin.
  2. Fry egg over medium heat. While cooking, add salt and pepper to taste
  3. Microwave sausage or bacon.
  4. Apply mayonaise to both slices of bread or english muffin, apply spicy sauce to one side.
  5. Stack egg and cooked meat to one slice of bread, and stack the last piece of bread on top.
