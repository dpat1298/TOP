Steak

Description

Here are some instructions on how to cook an amazingly glorious steak. What makes this steak so impressive is just how tender and juicy it is.

Ingredients

  • Steak
  • Salt
  • Montreal seasoning
  • Olive Oil

Steps

  1. Pull steak out of the refridgerator and sprinkle salt over both sides. We to allow the steak to warm up to room temperature.
  2. Turn stovetop on to medium heat. In a frying pan and add olive oil and place on burner.
  3. Place steak in pan
  4. Flip.
  5. When internal temperature is at least 145 degF, remove from pan and allow to rest for 10 mins.
